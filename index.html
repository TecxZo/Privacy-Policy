Because some stories are meant for later. Whether it’s a birthday message, a personal goal, or a heartfelt note — Time Capsules let you write for the future you. Open them on a special day and relive moments frozen in time.

Because DayStories is more than just a diary. It's your personal time machine — a place where you can capture memories, lock them away, and revisit them with intention.
